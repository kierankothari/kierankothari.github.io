About me

I hold an MSc in Computer Science from the University of Bath and a BA in Politics, Philosophy and Economics from Trinity College Dublin.

I am interested in data analytics and visualisations, and the insights that can be drawn from the information and data generated by people, organisations and the commercial market.

Below is a snapshot of my work. Using Python, SQLite and R, I have scrapped data from the popular online game Fantasy Premier League (FPL) in order to examine the trends and patterns from within the mini-league I play in.
