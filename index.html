About me

I hold a MSc in Computer Science from the University of Bath and a BA in Politics, Philosophy and Economics from Trinity College Dublin.

I am interested in data analytics and visualisations, and the insights that can be drawn from the information and data generated by people, organisations and the commercial market.

Below is a sample of my work. Using Python, SQLite and R, I have scrapped data from the popular online game Fantasy Premier League (FPL) in order to examine the trends and patterns from within the mini-league I play in.

  • CV

FPL is essentially an optimisation problem in which you must divide your assets (100m) across 15 footballers who have the potential to accrue points which contribute towards your overall score every week. Over 6 million players compete in the game globally, but you can also play against friends in smaller mini-leagues. The following is a presentation of the trends and patterns extracted from one of the 2018/19 mini-leagues I play in.
